<section><title>Creating a Doenet Account</title>  
<p>Creating an account is fast and easy!  To begin, go to <ref uri="https://doenet.org">doenet.org</ref> and click the "Sign In" button in the corner.</p>

  <image source='doenet:cid=bafkreid56ohl5iy2laol2445zk2tmjs3kn6dia6atqt32es46uqgbxfp54' description='screenshot of Doenet homepage showing Sign in button' asfilename='OnPaste.20240602-122019.png' size="large" mimeType='image/png' />
  
<p>Next, enter the email address you'd like to use for your account.  If you're using your own private computer, we suggest clicking the "Stay Logged In" button so that the site automatically remembers you.</p>

<image source='doenet:cid=bafkreia4voo74fqim2lfj7rrpqzs5avsmtkojhqtifgdz7bth62eknz3tm' description='doenet enter email' asfilename='Screenshot 2023-06-09 at 5.31.56 PM.png' width='500' aspectRatio="5/3" mimeType='image/png' />

<p>After clicking the "Send Email" button, check the email account that you just entered; you should receive an email from the Doenet server with a 9-digit access code.  Enter that code on screen and you'll be logged in!</p>

  <image source='doenet:cid=bafkreid37urwahefqcjjx24phj3qrqsnmhqygtgb2zkp5xperu57l572gu' description='doenet enter 9 digit code' asfilename='Screenshot 2023-06-09 at 5.32.56 PM.png' width='400' aspectRatio="400/292" mimeType='image/png' />

  
<p><alert>Note:</alert> If you want to log in again (say, from a different computer, or using a different browser), you'll go through the same process: enter your email address, and wait for a 9-digit access code.  The first time you log in and create an account, it may take a few minutes to receive the email with your access code.  For subsequent logins, the email should arrive almost immediately.</p>
</section>
