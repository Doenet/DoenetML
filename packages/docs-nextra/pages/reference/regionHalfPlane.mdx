# `<regionHalfPlane/>{:dn}`

<ComponentDisplay name='regionHalfPlane'/>

`<regionHalfPlane/>{:dn}` is a [Graphical](../concepts/essentialConcepts#component-types)
component that represents one side of the plane bounded by a vertical or horizontal line. It does not
render anything by itself; its role is to act as a region passed to a constraint such as
[`<constrainTo>`](constrainTo) or [`<constrainToInterior>`](constrainToInterior), restricting a
`<point>{:dn}` to that half-plane.

With the default attributes the region is the right half-plane $x \ge 0$.

<AttrPropDisplay name='regionHalfPlane'/>


## Examples

### Example: Constrain a `<point>{:dn}` to the right half-plane

```doenet-editor-horiz
<graph size="small" xmin="-5" xmax="5" ymin="-5" ymax="5">
  <shortDescription>A point that cannot cross to the left of the y-axis</shortDescription>
  <point name="P" styleNumber="2" coords="(2, 1)">
    <constrainTo>
      <regionHalfPlane />
    </constrainTo>
  </point>
</graph>

<p>P is at $P.coords.</p>
```

The point can be moved freely in the right half-plane and is held against the $y$-axis when dragged to
the left. The defaults — `horizontal="true"`, `boundaryValue="0"`, `greaterThan="true"` — describe the
half-plane $x \ge 0$.


## Attribute Examples

### Attribute Example: horizontal

```doenet-editor-horiz
<graph size="small" xmin="-5" xmax="5" ymin="-5" ymax="5">
  <shortDescription>A point that cannot move below the x-axis</shortDescription>
  <point name="P" styleNumber="2" coords="(1, 2)">
    <constrainTo>
      <regionHalfPlane horizontal="false" />
    </constrainTo>
  </point>
</graph>
```

When `horizontal` is `false`, the half-plane extends vertically instead of horizontally. Combined
with the default `boundaryValue="0"` and `greaterThan="true"`, this is the upper half-plane $y \ge 0$.


---

### Attribute Example: boundaryValue

```doenet-editor-horiz
<graph size="small" xmin="-6" xmax="6" ymin="-6" ymax="6">
  <shortDescription>A point constrained to lie at or to the right of x = 2</shortDescription>
  <point name="P" styleNumber="2" coords="(4, 0)">
    <constrainTo>
      <regionHalfPlane boundaryValue="2" />
    </constrainTo>
  </point>
</graph>
```

`boundaryValue` shifts the bounding line. With `horizontal="true"` (the default), it sets the vertical
boundary $x = 2$.


---

### Attribute Example: greaterThan

```doenet-editor-horiz
<graph size="small" xmin="-6" xmax="6" ymin="-6" ymax="6">
  <shortDescription>A point constrained to the upper half-plane y less than or equal to 0</shortDescription>
  <point name="P" styleNumber="2" coords="(1, -2)">
    <constrainTo>
      <regionHalfPlane horizontal="false" greaterThan="false" />
    </constrainTo>
  </point>
</graph>
```

Setting `greaterThan` to `false` selects the opposite side of the boundary. The combination of
`horizontal="false"` and `greaterThan="false"` here gives the lower half-plane $y \le 0$.
