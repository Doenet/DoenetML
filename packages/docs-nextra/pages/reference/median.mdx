# ` <median>{:dn}`

` <median>{:dn}` is a Math Operator component which renders the median of the enclosed values.


## Attributes and Properties 
 <AttrDisplay name='median'/> 
 <PropDisplay name='median'/>

---

### Example: ` <median>{:dn}` of a sugared list of numbers


```doenet-editor-horiz
<p><m>\mathbb{A} = \{1, 2, 3, -1\}</m></p>
<p>The median of <m>\mathbb{A}= <median>1 2 3 -1</median></m></p>
```



Numbers are listed directly within opening and closing tags of the ` <median>{:dn}` component. The [` <m>{:dn}`](m.mdx) component is used for mathematical desplay.



---

### Example: ` <median>{:dn}` of named <tage>mathInput</tage>s


```doenet-editor-horiz
<p>Choose three numbers: </p>
<p><m>x = </m> <mathInput name="x"/></p>
<p><m>y = </m> <mathInput name="y"/></p> 
<p><m>z = </m> <mathInput name="z"/></p>
<p>  
The median of your three numbers <m>\{$x, $y, $z\}</m> is:  <number><median>$x $y $z</median></number>
</p>
```



In this example, three <ref uri="doenet:activityId=_p_qPrnY-jhcqqrG2LcodH">` <mathInput/>{:dn}`</ref>s are referenced using the `$name` notation within the ` <median>{:dn}` component.
