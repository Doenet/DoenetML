# `<nbsp/>{:dn}`


` <nbsp/>{:dn}` renders white space in a paragraph.

---

### Example: <tage>nbsp</tage> in a paragraph


```doenet-editor-horiz
<p>The time has come, the Walrus said, to talk of many things,</p>
<p>Of shoes,</p>
<p><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/>and ships,</p>
<p><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/>and ceiling wax...</p>
<p>Of carpenters,</p>
<p><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/>and kings.</p>
```



The ` <nbsp/>{:dn}` component is used to force additional white space where desired in a paragraph.
