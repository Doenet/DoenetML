# `<nbsp/>{:dn}`


` <nbsp/>{:dn}` is a [Paragraph Markup](../document_structure/essentialConcepts#component-types) 
component that renders white space in a paragraph.


## Attributes and Properties 
The `<nbsp/>{:dn}` component does not have attributes.
 <PropDisplay name='nbsp'/>


---

### Example: `<nbsp/>{:dn}` in a paragraph


```doenet-editor-horiz
<p>The time has come, the Walrus said, to talk of many things,</p>
<p>Of shoes,</p>
<p><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/>and ships,</p>
<p><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/>
    and ceiling wax...</p>
<p>Of carpenters,</p>
<p><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/><nbsp/>
    and kings.</p>
```



The ` <nbsp/>{:dn}` component is used to force additional white space where desired in a paragraph.
