# ` <mean>{:dn}`

` <mean>{:dn}` is a Math Operator component which renders the arithmetic mean of the enclosed values.

## Attributes and Properties 
 <AttrDisplay name='mean'/> 
 <PropDisplay name='mean'/>

---

### Example: ` <mean>{:dn}` of a sugared list of numbers


```doenet-editor-horiz
<p><m>\mathbb{A} = \{1, 2, 3, -1\}</m></p>
<p>The mean of <m>\mathbb{A}= <mean>1 2 3 -1</mean></m></p>
```



Numbers are listed directly within opening and closing tags of the ` <mean>{:dn}` component. The [` <m>{:dn}`](m.mdx) component is used for mathematical desplay.



---

### Example: ` <mean>{:dn}` of named <tage>mathInput</tage>s


```doenet-editor-horiz
<p>Choose three numbers: </p>
<p><m>x = </m> <mathInput name="x"/></p>
<p><m>y = </m> <mathInput name="y"/></p> 
<p><m>z = </m> <mathInput name="z"/></p>
<p>  
The mean of your three numbers <m>\{$x, $y, $z\}</m> is:  <number><mean>$x $y $z</mean></number>
</p>
```



In this example, three <ref uri="doenet:activityId=_p_qPrnY-jhcqqrG2LcodH">` <mathInput/>{:dn}`</ref>s are referenced using the `$name` notation within the ` <mean>{:dn}` component.
